Terms of Use — Its Story Time

Last updated: April 12, 2026

1. About the Skill

Its Story Time is a free Alexa skill that narrates Indian folklore stories in English and Hindi. Stories include tales from Panchatantra, Akbar-Birbal, Vikram-Betaal, Tenali Raman, Jataka Tales, and other Indian traditions.

2. Content

All stories are original retellings of traditional Indian folklore that are in the public domain. The content is family-friendly and suitable for all ages. Stories are provided for entertainment and educational purposes.
