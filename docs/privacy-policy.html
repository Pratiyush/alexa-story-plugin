Privacy Policy — Its Story Time

Last updated: April 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Its Story Time does not collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not require account linking or any login credentials.

2. Data Usage

The skill uses only the following data provided by Amazon Alexa during each session:

  • Locale — to determine language preference (English or Hindi)
  • Session attributes — to remember your language choice and current story during a single session. This data is not stored after the session ends.

3. Data Storage

No user data is stored persistently. Session attributes exist only for the duration of your interaction with the skill and are automatically deleted when the session ends.

4. Third-Party Services

This skill runs on Amazon Web Services (AWS Lambda) as part of the Alexa Skills platform. Please refer to Amazon's Alexa Terms of Use for information about how Amazon handles your data.

5. Children's Privacy

This skill is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at github.com/Pratiyush/alexa-story-plugin.
